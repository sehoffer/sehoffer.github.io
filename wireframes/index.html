Final Project: Portfolio Website

User Profiles

1. Employer

They want to view my work and get a feel for my aesthetic and work ethic.

They got to my site through a link I sent them when applying for a job.

They're interested in:

  • A well designed site
  • High-res images
  • Project descriptions
  • Artist statement
  • Contact information
  • Resume

2. Family

They want to see the work i'm doing at school and be able to oooh and aaah at it.

They asked where they are able to see my work and received a link from me.

They're interested in:

  • Images
  • Project descriptions
  • Easy navigation
  • A way to comment on, "like", or save images

3. Other art students

They want inspiration for projects they are doing.

They found my website by randomly surfing the web.

They're interested in:

  • Images
  • Project descriptions
  • Inspiration/concept
  • What school I went to
  • Other portfolio websites
